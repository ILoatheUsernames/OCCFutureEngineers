About OCC's Future Engineers

This fictional engineering firm was created by the LSU Olinde Career Center to help students in the College of Engineering prepare for real-world interviews. Through mock opportunities like this, students sharpen their communication, technical knowledge, and confidence.

Our Mission & Values

Mission: To engineer opportunity, empower students, and model the professionalism expected in the global engineering industry.

  • Integrity – We conduct every project with academic and professional honesty.
  • Innovation – We believe creativity drives meaningful engineering solutions.
  • Mentorship – We grow future engineers through hands-on learning and coaching.
  • Impact – We challenge students to solve problems that matter to people and communities.

Mock Internship Description

Title: Engineer Intern

Location: Baton Rouge, LA (Simulated)

As an intern with OCC's Future Engineers, you’ll gain insight into structural analysis, mechanical systems design, project documentation, and professional teamwork. This opportunity supports the Mock Interview Program at LSU and is not a real position.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Analyze, design, and evaluate engineering systems
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams and contribute to simulated fieldwork
  • Practice using CAD software, drafting techniques, and technical reporting
  • Engage in root cause analysis, inspection, and process improvement

Qualifications:

  • LSU Engineering student approved for Fall 2025 Mock Interview participation
  • Interest in structural, electrical, or mechanical engineering
  • Strong communication and problem-solving skills

Why Join OCC's Future Engineers?

  • Get hands-on experience with project-based engineering challenges
  • Mentorship from industry-experienced professionals
  • Collaborate in a supportive, inclusive, and innovation-focused environment
  • Build skills that translate into future internships and full-time careers
